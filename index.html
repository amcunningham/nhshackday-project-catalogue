UK Vaccination Eligibility Checker

Find out which NHS vaccines you were (or are) eligible for, based on your date of birth and sex. Currently covers anyone born from 1980 onwards.

Important: This tool is for informational purposes only and reflects the routine UK immunisation schedule. It does not account for individual risk factors, occupational vaccines, travel vaccines, or targeted programmes (e.g. neonatal BCG, hepatitis B for at-risk groups). The schedule shown is based on when vaccines were part of the routine NHS programme in England. Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland broadly follow the same schedule but may have minor timing differences. Always consult your GP or check your personal health record for definitive information.
